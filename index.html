Stand Alone Senior High Schools (SHS) in Taguig are public schools that offer Grades 11 and 12 education, separate from regular high schools. These schools cater to students who want to pursue specialized tracks like Academic, Technical-Vocational-Livelihood (TVL), Sports, or Arts and Design.
